Service Conditions

Last updated: June 3, 2026

These conditions describe operational limits and expected behavior when using PIC Check AI.

1. Accuracy Conditions

Scores can be affected by screenshots, compression, cropping, filters, re-uploads, camera captures of screens, illustrations, watermarks, low resolution, and provider quota limits.

2. Provider Fallback

The service may switch between configured providers. If trusted providers are unavailable or rate-limited, browser-based heuristics may be used and will be less accurate.

3. Storage Conditions

Member history may store compressed images and thumbnails rather than original full-resolution files. Duplicate scans may be skipped based on file hash.

4. Account Conditions

Member features require Google sign-in. You are responsible for keeping access to your Google account secure.

5. Availability

The service may be changed, suspended, rate-limited, or discontinued at any time. Maintenance or third-party provider issues may affect availability.

6. Reporting Issues

If provider limits or incorrect results affect your workflow, contact johnlahboo@gmail.com or Telegram @movewannamove.
